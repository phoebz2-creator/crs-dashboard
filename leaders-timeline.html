Overview

This page tracks major diplomatic visits, bilateral meetings, and summit engagements involving leaders from China, the United States, Japan, South Korea, major European countries, Russia, and Ukraine.

The timeline is intended to provide a structured view of high-level diplomacy, strategic partnerships, and geopolitical developments through leader-to-leader interactions.
